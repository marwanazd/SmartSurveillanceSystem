How to Use the System

The Smart Surveillance System is designed to provide an easy and efficient way to monitor your premises. Here is a step-by-step guide to using the system:

  1. Access the Dashboard: Log in to your account and navigate to the dashboard to view all connected cameras in real-time.
  2. Control Cameras: Use the controls to turn cameras on/off, adjust brightness, and change resolution.
  3. Live Streaming: Click on any camera feed to view the live stream in fullscreen mode.
  4. Set Motion Detection: Enable motion detection for specific cameras to get alerts for unusual activities.
  5. Recording Schedule: Schedule recording times for your cameras to optimize storage and focus on critical periods.
  6. Logs and Alerts: Access the logs to review activity history and set up alerts for specific events.
  7. Manage Users: Add or remove users who can access the system and define their permissions.

For a detailed walkthrough of features, visit the dashboard or check out the settings page to customize the system to your preferences.
